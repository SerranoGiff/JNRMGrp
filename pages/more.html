About Us

Our Journey: From Humble Beginnings to Global Reach

At JNRM Corporation, heart and passion are at the foundation of everything we do. What began as a small family business with just ₱800 has blossomed into a global leader in premium food products, creating authentic, original flavors that satisfy palates worldwide.

With over three decades of dedication, JNRM continues to innovate, grow, and impact communities by delivering quality products that meet international standards.

A Legacy of Quality and Innovation

For generations, we’ve honed our craft, combining traditional techniques with modern innovations to produce a wide range of products. From frozen foods, beverages, and dry mixes to desserts and more, we ensure that every product is crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients. Our brands are trusted by home cooks, professional chefs, and renowned restaurants both in the Philippines and across borders.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality products that meet global standards. Our investment in cutting-edge technology and continuous human resource development reflects our desire to stay at the forefront of the food industry. From food safety certifications to sustainable practices, we ensure that every step in our production process is carried out with integrity and precision.

Supporting Local Communities

JNRM's passion extends beyond our business, as we actively support local farmers and communities. By sourcing ingredients from sustainable Philippine farms, we not only create exceptional products but also contribute to the livelihoods of thousands of Filipino families. We believe in uplifting communities while bringing the true taste of the Philippines to the world.
